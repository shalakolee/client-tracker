# Product Context

## Why it exists
ClientTracker is a client/sales/commission tracking app; it needs a clean, consistent, modern UX.

## Problems it solves
- Manage clients, contacts, sales, and commission schedules.
- View dashboards and calendar-style commission views.

## UX goals
- Follow Material 3 standards for layout, lists, inputs, sheets, icons, and motion.
- Android-first visual correctness, dark/light theme parity.
- Stable navigation (back swipes, sheets, filters).
